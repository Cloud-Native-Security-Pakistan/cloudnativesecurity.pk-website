Brand Guidelines

Our logo represents our brand and helps people recognize us at a glance. As a key part of our identity, it should be used consistently.

Logo

We call our logo the Shield — it represents cloud native security with a modern tech aesthetic. The shield combines cloud infrastructure elements with a security lock, symbolizing our mission to protect cloud native environments.

Primary Logo

The Shield

The standalone shield may be used in CNSP-owned environments or where the brand is already clearly established. It's our primary mark and represents the community across all platforms.

Wordmark Lockup

We use the lockup, featuring the shield and wordmark, in most places. This logo represents the brand in professional environments for easy recognition.

Typography

Cloud Native Security Pakistan is always written with proper capitalization. When referring to the community, organization, or initiative, keep it simple — CNSP or Cloud Native Security Pakistan.

Color Variants

The Shield and our wordmark should only appear in white, black, or our brand colors. Like text, the logo should be legible and pass accessibility requirements in all settings.

Usage Guidelines

The logo is our most important graphic device. It should sit high in the visual hierarchy — large, with high contrast and no other effects.

Do these things
  • Use approved logo files only
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the logo
  • Use high contrast color combinations
  • Scale proportionally
  • Use on solid backgrounds
Don't do these things
  • Don't stretch or distort the logo
  • Don't add shadows or effects
  • Don't change the logo colors
  • Don't rotate the logo
  • Don't place on busy backgrounds
